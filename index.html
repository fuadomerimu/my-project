In addition to providing a premium product, we aim to enhance the customer experience by developing a delivery app that streamlines the process of purchasing khat in Addis Ababa.

Market Size Overview: The daily influx of khat to Addis Ababa from various sources is estimated at 70 million Birr. This translates to an annual khat consumption exceeding 25,500,000,000 Birr in Addis Ababa alone. This substantial figure underscores the vast untapped potential within the khat market.

Market Objective: Our primary goal is to secure a significant market share, targeting a 20% penetration within the khat market in Addis Ababa. We are confident that through a well-executed strategy, our company can achieve this ambitious objective.

Projected Impact: Upon reaching our market share target, our company anticipates selling over 200,000 qat per day. This level of sales is expected to generate an annual revenue exceeding 1,440,000,000 Birr, illustrating the substantial potential for success and profitability in the khat market. Our commitment to quality and innovation positions us to become a key player in the industry, meeting the demands of our customers and contributing to the growth of the market.
